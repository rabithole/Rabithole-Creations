Oowee Gooey Brownies

This was my first ever project to go live on the web. It involved some very specific instructions from the client who had a Graphic Designer work with me in its design. They are located in Southern California and required a trip there to take all the photos in a two day span. All processing was done by me. Overall I am satisfied with them. I learned a lot with this experience and look forward to applying what I learned toward my next product photography venture. I utilized squareup.com for their online store. I hope to learn to create custom online stores in the future.

Triple "M" Alpacas

This is the second site I created. A family run operation that simply enjoys the lifestyle. Alpaca's were advertised as a great investment opportunity that died in the 2008 banking crash. I learned a lot about CSS when creating this site. In particular, how the mouse hover event could be used to expose hidden objects. This was the first time I used a photograph as a background picture for a site. What I learned here I have applied to responsive design techniques.
